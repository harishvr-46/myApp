5's Pledge

I as an Employee of DHI, will maintain my workplace in an organized manner by:

  1. Segregating and setting things in Place.

  2. Keeping my workplace neat & clean.

  3. Following the standards for sustaining the work culture.

DHI MISSION : Enabling Informed Decisions.

DHI VISION : DHI Stands for excellence practices across stakeholders towards achieving 'Fine structured Constant'.

What is 5S?

Sort: To remove what is not needed and keep only what is needed. Question why we accumulated so much for so many days.

Set In Order: Place for everything and everything in its place. Identification and labelling. Keep only minimum quantity at the point of use.

Shine: Keep things clean and polished. No trash or dirt in the workplace. "don't clean, keep it clean.......Cleaning with meaning."

Standardize: Establish Standards for 1st 3S(1S, 2S, 3S). Use visual methods to expose waste and abnormalities."

Self-Discipline: Sustain through continuous training & Self Discipline. Make 5s as a culture.
